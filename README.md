# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard

An interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard** developed using **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, employee satisfaction, and other important HR metrics. The dashboard enables HR professionals to monitor workforce trends and make data-driven decisions for improving employee retention and organizational performance.

# 📌 Project Overview

Employee attrition is a major concern for organizations as it directly impacts productivity, recruitment costs, and overall business growth.

This project transforms raw HR employee data into meaningful visual insights using Power BI. The dashboard provides a centralized view of workforce statistics, helping HR teams understand employee behavior and identify factors contributing to attrition.



# 🎯 Problem Statement

The HR department requires an interactive dashboard to monitor workforce performance and employee attrition effectively.

The dashboard aims to solve the following business problems:

- Monitor total employee count
- Track employee attrition
- Calculate attrition rate
- Identify active employees
- Analyze average employee age
- Compare attrition across departments
- Study workforce distribution by age
- Analyze education background of employees leaving the organization
- Evaluate employee job satisfaction
- Compare attrition between male and female employees

# 📈 Dashboard Visualizations

## Employee Overview

Displays important HR KPIs including:

- Total Employees
- Attrition Count
- Attrition Rate
- Active Employees
- Average Employee Age



## Department-wise Attrition

Visualizes employee attrition across departments.

**Purpose**

- Identify departments with higher turnover
- Support targeted employee retention strategies



## Number of Employees by Age Group

Displays workforce distribution across different age groups along with gender.

**Purpose**

- Analyze workforce demographics
- Compare male and female employee distribution
- Identify age group concentration



## Job Satisfaction Rating

Shows satisfaction ratings across different job roles.

**Purpose**

- Measure employee satisfaction
- Identify job roles requiring HR attention
- Improve employee engagement



## Education Field-wise Attrition

Displays attrition count based on employees' educational backgrounds.

**Purpose**

- Identify education fields with higher attrition
- Understand workforce educational trends



## Attrition Rate by Gender for Different Age Groups

Displays gender-wise attrition across different age categories.

**Purpose**

- Analyze gender-specific turnover
- Identify age groups with higher employee exits
- Support targeted retention strategies
